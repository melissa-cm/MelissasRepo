About me

I am an Industrial Engineer with a Masters degree in Social Development Studies; specialised in quantitative, qualitative and mixed methods research. With more than three years of experience, I have worked on a range of interdisciplinary research projects with British, North-American and Colombian research agencies and higher education institutions; through which I have gained meaningful knowledge in survey design, validity and reliability, multivariate statistical analysis and quantitiative data processing. In qualitative research, my work has focused on discourse analysis, qualitative coding techniques and focus groups to better understand people's experiences, motivations and insights.

I am intellectually curious, detail-oriented and passionate about qualitative and quantitative data analysis.
